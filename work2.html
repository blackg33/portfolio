Chorus In the Forest

About the project:

In a team of 4, we designed and built a music festival site with a CMS for easy content management. I used the Adobe Creative Suite to edit images for my JQuery slider and designed an easy-to-use CRUD page where admins can upload new images, and change details such as where the images link to.

Technologies:

Stripe, HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, JQuery, PHP, MYSQL

My roles:

Design, Ticket purchasing with Stripe integration, Image Slider, CMS for the two features
